# Recipes

A **Recipe** is a short, focused answer to a single modeling question. Recipes are the chapter to consult when you have a domain in hand, hit a specific problem, and want a directly applicable solution.

This chapter complements **[Concepts](/concepts/overview.md)** (which explains the vocabulary) and **[Modeling Guides](/modeling-guides/overview.md)** (which walks through entire example domains). A Recipe stands alone: it makes sense on its own, doesn't require reading other Recipes first, and is short enough to read in a few minutes.

## Available Recipes

Recipes are added over time, driven by real questions that come up in practice. The first Recipes will follow the first Modeling Guide – several questions naturally surface during a walkthrough, and the Recipes are where the answers live in a form you can search and reference later.

If you have a question you'd like to see covered as a Recipe, write to **[hello@thenativeweb.io](mailto:hello@thenativeweb.io)**.
